Resources

Here are some of our favorite resources for organizing, productivity, and product reviews. We hope you find them helpful!

  • Get Organized Now - Offers thousands of clutter-busting ideas, time management tips, and organizing systems.
  • Organized Home - Provides actionable organizing strategies for specific areas like kitchens, bathrooms, and pantries.
  • A Bowl Full of Lemons - Focuses on home and family organization, with sections on office organization, emergency preparedness, and DIY projects.
  • The Home Edit - A go-to resource for home organization, offering tools, tips, and inspiration for transforming spaces.
  • Consumer Reports - Provides unbiased reviews and ratings for over 7.7 million products, including organizing tools like storage bins and kitchen appliances.
  • Todoist - A task management app trusted by 30 million users, ideal for clients looking to organize tasks and projects.
  • Milanote - A visual tool for organizing ideas and projects into boards.
