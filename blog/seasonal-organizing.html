Seasonal Organizing: Preparing Your Home for Spring/Summer/Fall/Winter

Each season brings its own organizing challenges. Here are some tips to help you stay on top of things all year round:

Spring

Spring is the perfect time for a deep clean and declutter. Go through your closets and donate any clothes you no longer wear. And don't forget to clean out your garage and shed.

Summer

Summer is all about enjoying the outdoors. Get your patio and backyard ready for entertaining. And if you have kids, create a system for organizing all their summer toys and gear.

Fall

As the weather cools down, it's time to get your home ready for winter. Clean your gutters, check your furnace, and stock up on emergency supplies. For more fall organizing tips, check out the holiday section of Get Organized Now.

Winter

Winter is the perfect time to tackle indoor organizing projects. Organize your holiday decorations, declutter your basement, and create a cozy and inviting home for the holidays.
