Mindful Organizing: How to Let Go of Sentimental Items

Letting go of sentimental items can be one of the biggest challenges of decluttering. Here are some strategies to help you through the process:

  • Ask Yourself "Why?": Why are you holding on to this item? Is it because it brings you joy, or because you feel guilty getting rid of it?
  • Take a Picture: If you're having trouble letting go of an item, take a picture of it. This can help you preserve the memory without having to keep the physical object.
  • The "Spark Joy" Method: Marie Kondo's "spark joy" method can be a great way to decide what to keep and what to let go of. For more on this, check out A Bowl Full of Lemons.
  • Start Small: Don't try to tackle all your sentimental items at once. Start with a small box and work your way up.
  • Give Yourself Grace: It's okay to have a hard time letting go. Be patient with yourself and remember that it's a process.
